Hirano Sho

1. Basic Information

Hirano Sho is a Japanese idol and actor. He was born in Nagoya in Aichi, Japan, on the 29th January, 1997 (age 26). He is so handsome. He does not like to eat shiitake mushroom(me too). He gave himself an English name called "Flat Space" and his favourite English word is "Octopus".

2. Career

In 2010 to 2011, Hirano was belonged to an entertainment agency company in Nagoya, and was a member of a local idol group called BOYS AND MEN, then joined Johnny's Associated as a member of Johnny's Jr. from 2012. In 2018, he debuted as a member of the idol group "King & Prince". However, he lefted in 22th May, 2023, and entered a new company called TOBE, and formed a new group "Number_i" with two other members of King & Prince.
